Tiko Lin is an interactive art student, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science Degree in Interactive Arts and Technology program from Simon Fraser University. She was born and raised in a small coastal city of China where she built up an intimate connection and bond with nature that she is able to get inspiration from. She felt that interactivity is all about this connection between objects and people. If the artwork touches or inspires you and talks to you, it is interactive. It resembles nature, where everything is alive and has a story. Tiko has experiences and skills with graphic design, prototyping, and programming. She is passionate about the way art combines with technology and bring each other to life.
