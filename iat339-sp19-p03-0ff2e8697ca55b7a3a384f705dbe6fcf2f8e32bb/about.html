My name is Tiko, an interactive art student, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science Degree in Interactive Arts and Technology program from Simon Fraser University. As a design student, I love doing sketches using pencil, and that is the best way to come up with good designs for me. I like vivid and bold colours and designs. If the design touches, inspires or talks to you, it is interactive. I felt that interactivity is all about connection between objects and people. I have experiences and skills with graphic design, prototyping, and programing. I am passionate about the way art combines with technology and bring each other to life.
